Admission Process

Admissions take place from November to March.
Please visit the campus or click below button to submit your Query.
Inquiry

Documents Required for Nursery to Standard 1st :

1. Registration Form
2. Attested Birth Certificate
3. Proof of Residence
4. 3 passport size photographs each of the child, father and mother

Documents Required for standard 1st to Standard 12th :

1. Registration Form
2. School Leaving Certificate
3. Attested xerox copy of academic report card from the previous school attended.
4. Proof of Residence
5. 3 passport size photographs each of the child, father and mother

Admission Process:

Step 1:
Visit the campus
Collect The Registration Form
Step 2:
Complete & Submit Registration Form
Enclose the required documents ( Birth certificate for Nursery to standard 1st)
(School Leaving Certificate & attested xerox copy of Report card from the last school attended for Standard 2nd to Standard)
Step 3:
Wait for notification from the school
Step 4:
On being notified, Pay the fee
Accept congratulatory letter and Collect information for next session
